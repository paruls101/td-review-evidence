  • Verified review
    If you are being recommended to have a osteotomy, all I can say is that a double osteotomy carried out by Mr Chattoo 15 months ago, dramatically improved my life. In the run up to my operation I was in a lot of pain. I could barely walk 50m without a walking stick, and not much further with one. Mr Chatoo was clear when we met, that historic damage to my knee and subsequent repair work carried out 40 years ago after a motorbike accident, might mean the operation could not be done, or if done, might be of limited benefit. I was at my wits end and, in desperation, had even asked whether amputation was a potential solution (apparently an unusual question). But thankfully after consulting with colleagues, Mr Chatoo agreed to go ahead with a double osteotomy in June 2024. I was astonished with the results from the start. The operation was on a Friday. The next day I was able to stand with crutches and very limited weight baring. I was sent home with a voucher for an ice-machine which arrived immediately. I used this for 3-6 hours a day whilst in bed or on the sofa, and attribute to it the low level of pain and discomfort I experienced. I completed the daily exercises I’d been given religiously, and at my 6 week appointment was able to walk, first with only one crutch (I’d practiced the day before), and then no crutches. I walked out of the appointment without crutches, though took them home ‘just in case’. At the 12 week appointment, having continued with the recommended daily exercises, I was able not only do a star jump but to leap, two-footed, towards Mr Chatoo. I haven’t been able to leap confidently in 40 years, and yet here I was doing exactly that, just 12 weeks after the operation. After that I joined a gym and for 12 weeks focused on leg exercises for 1 hour, 3 times a week. Within 3 months I was leg pressing 110kg and squatting 40kg, something I’d never done before. A year on we’ve just returned from a holiday during which I walked a little over 100 miles. All with absolutely no pain or discomfort. The double osteotomy has changed my life. It was without doubt the best thing I could have done. I write this not only written to help you consider having the operation, but also as a way to express my heart-felt gratitude for the exceptional efforts of Mr Chatoo and his team. His expertise and attention to every detail undoubtedly made a significant contribution to my recovery. Beyond his evidently impressive skills as a surgeon, I appreciated his passion, patience and humour – as well as his willingness to answer all my questions, including the one about amputation!
